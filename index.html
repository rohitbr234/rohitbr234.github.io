Intro

Hi, I’m Rohit – a high school student who loves exploring how data, AI, and human behavior intersect. I’m always looking for ways to apply what I learn to real-world problems.

Projects

(May 2024 - Nov 2024) Urban Heat Island Analysis: I used satellite imagery and machine learning to model urban heat island intensity across cities worldwide, helping understand how urban design affects local temperatures.

(Dec 2024 - Aug 2025) EEG Analysis with Machine Learning: I analyzed open-access EEG datasets using unsupervised machine learning, incorporating electrode mapping and clustering techniques to explore patterns in meditation expertise. Presented findings at an undergraduate research conference.

(Jan 2025 - Jul 2025) HRV Analysis: I designed and ran an experiment measuring heart rate variability before and after mindful breathing, collecting and processing blood pressure and EKG data. Shared results through community outreach.

(Jun 2025 - Present) Short-form Video Clipper: I developed a script to convert long-form videos into short-form clips, automatically generating captions, formatting subtitles, and preparing content for platforms like YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram Reels.

About

I’m a rising senior passionate about computer science, environmental science, and psychology. Whether it’s coding AI models to analyze satellite data or designing experiments to study mindfulness, I enjoy projects that combine analytical thinking with curiosity about how the world works. Outside of academics, I like reading about neuroscience, working on small automation scripts, and learning new programming frameworks.
